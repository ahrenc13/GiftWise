## What to Do When Approvals Arrive

Auto-approvals typically come within 24-48h. Manual reviews take 3-7 days.

**When a merchant approves:**
1. Check their product feed availability (see `feedEnabled` column — yes = product feed exists)
2. Merchants with `feedEnabled: yes` can potentially be wired into the search pipeline
3. Merchants without feeds need static product lists (like existing CJ partners in `cj_searcher.py`)
4. High-priority to wire first: Quilling Card, Twisted Lily, Name Stories, Scribble, Crown and Paw, Farmgirl Flowers

**Merchants with product feeds (feedEnabled: yes) — prioritize for pipeline integration:**
- Crown and Paw, BroBasket, Napkins.com, Darn Good Yarn, Sports Box Co.,
  Scribble, Miss to Mrs Box, Swanky Badger, Woven Woven, Grill Masters Club,
  Jasper Hill Farm, Sugarwish, CanvasChamp, LoveIsARose.com, VitaJuwel,

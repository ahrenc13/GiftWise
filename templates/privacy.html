Giftwise Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 22, 2026

What We Collect

Giftwise collects data from your connected social media platforms (Pinterest, YouTube, Instagram, TikTok) to generate personalized gift recommendations. This includes your pins, boards, watch history, posts, and liked content.

How We Use Your Data

We use your social media data solely to analyze your interests and generate personalized gift recommendations. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers and is only accessible by you. You can disconnect any platform and delete your data at any time through your account settings.

Third Party Services

We use official APIs from Pinterest, YouTube, and other platforms to access your data with your explicit permission. We comply with all terms of service from these platforms.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data at any time
  • Delete your data and account
  • Disconnect platforms whenever you want
  • Request a copy of your data

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All connections use secure HTTPS protocols and OAuth authentication.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at: privacy@giftwise.fit

## Phase C3: Next Reel

**Status:** Not started — determined by C2 signal

### Candidate concepts (pick based on C2 data)

**"The Boyfriend Problem"**
- Hook: "My boyfriend is impossible to shop for. I tried this." (first 2 seconds)
- Shows: pasting his Instagram → watching results come in → specific gift that fits him perfectly
- Why: "gift ideas for boyfriend" is one of highest-searched gift queries
- Format: UGC-style, phone-in-hand, casual

**"The Weird Interest Test"**
- Hook: "What if the person is obsessed with [niche thing]. Watch." (pick something specific — taxidermy, competitive eating, urban exploration)
- Shows: AI handling specificity — this is the differentiator vs generic gift lists
- Why: Specificity is the product's actual advantage. Show it working on a hard case.
- Format: Screen recording + voiceover

**"POV: You're the gift recipient"**
- Hook: "POV: someone ran your Instagram through an AI to buy you a gift"
- Shows: Results page from recipient's POV — "wait, how does it know I love this?"
- Why: Ego-bait — everyone wants to see their own gift list. Highly shareable.
- Format: Screen recording, text overlays, no face needed
- **This is probably the highest-potential concept for virality**

## Phase C4: Comment Reply Series

**Status:** Not started — triggered by C1 or C3 engagement

### Format
- Reply to a comment with "Replying to @user" stitch
- Each reply is 15-30 seconds
- Answers specific use case: "does it work for dads?" "what about long-distance relationships?"
- Each reply extends the original's reach AND becomes standalone content

### High-value comment types to reply to
- "Does this work for [specific person]?" → make that your next reel
- "Is it free?" → quick demo reel showing the flow
- "What if they don't have social media?" → address the limitation honestly

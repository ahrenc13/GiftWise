# Content Workstream — Distribution Strategy

**Goal:** Get 50 real `rec_run` events from strangers. Content is the primary distribution lever.

**Two audiences, two formats:**
- **Your network (Facebook/Instagram):** Casual, authentic, self-deprecating. No production value needed. Posts, not videos.
- **TikTok cold audience:** Your kid's video OR the polished reel. Different asset, different ask.

**How I keep you on task:** At the start of sessions, tell me where you are in the content workstream and I'll tell you the next action. Or just ask "what's next in content?" and I'll pull this file and tell you exactly what to do.

---

## Phase C0: Your Own Network — Post Now

**Status:** Ready to execute — no dependencies, no production needed
**Gate:** Post live on your personal Facebook/Instagram

This is the fastest path to first real users. Your network trusts you. A stranger on TikTok doesn't.

### The Post (copy-paste ready)

> I've been quietly building something for the last few months. It's an AI gift finder — you give it someone's Instagram or TikTok handle and it reads their actual posts to figure out what they'd love. No questionnaires, no generic lists.
>
> It's at giftwise.fit if you want to try it. I'm 50 and I built it, so manage your expectations accordingly.
>
> If you have a birthday coming up for someone impossible to shop for, try it and tell me what you think.

**Why this works:** Self-deprecating, specific ask ("birthday coming up"), your real face behind it. People click things their friends made.

### Checklist
- [ ] Post to Facebook
- [ ] Post to Instagram (can be same text or shorter)
- [ ] Reply to every single comment personally — this is how you learn what people want
- [ ] Note: what did people say? Did anyone try it?

### After posting
Tell me what the comments said. I'll help you figure out what to do next.

## Phase C2: 48h Signal Read

**Status:** Not started — starts after C1 posts
**Gate:** 48 hours after C1 posts

### What to look at
- View count and velocity (did it plateau fast or keep climbing?)
- Comment themes: what are people saying? What questions are they asking?
- Profile visits and site clicks from bio link
- Saves (saves = people bookmarking it to use later — very good signal)

### Decision tree
| Signal | Next move |
|--------|-----------|
| < 500 views, no engagement | Different hook — try "The Boyfriend Problem" concept |
| 500-2k views, some comments | Make 1-2 more in same style, reply to top comments with stitches |
| 2k+ views | Reply to comments, make "POV: Your Instagram" next |
| Any comment asking "does this work for X?" | That IS your next reel |
| Your kid's video gets more traction | Let them run with it, you support |

### How to report back
Tell me the view count at 24h and 48h, and paste any interesting comments. I'll tell you the next move.

## Phase C3: Your Kid's Video

**Status:** Pending — their call, not yours
**Gate:** They decide to make it

### What to do (your job)
- Let them watch the reel you made so they know what the product does
- Give them a free run at their own concept — don't script it
- The only ask: make sure giftwise.fit is in the video or caption

### What not to do
- Don't give them a storyboard
- Don't ask them to make it "professional"
- Don't post it yourself — they should post it from their account

### Why this works differently than your reel
A teenager posting about their parent's AI project is a different kind of content — it's not a product demo, it's a story. "My dad built this" gets shared in a way that "check out this app" doesn't.

## Phase C4: Next Reel (after C2 signal)

**Status:** Not started — concept chosen based on C2 data

### Candidate concepts

**"The Boyfriend Problem"** — High search intent
- Hook: "My boyfriend is impossible to shop for. I tried this." (first 2 seconds)
- Format: Phone-in-hand, UGC-style, casual

**"The Weird Interest Test"** — Shows the differentiator
- Hook: "What if the person is obsessed with [something niche]. Watch."
- Format: Screen recording + voiceover
- Pick a genuinely weird interest — the more specific, the better the demo

**"POV: You're the gift recipient"** — Highest viral potential
- Hook: "POV: someone ran your Instagram through an AI to buy you a gift"
- Format: Screen recording, text overlays, no face needed
- Why: ego-bait — everyone wants to see their own list. Highly shareable.

### Maintenance Rules

1. **Urgency banners**: Add shipping deadline callouts 6 weeks before each holiday — they drive conversions
2. **"Updated [Month Year]"**: Refresh the date tag whenever content is touched — signals freshness to Google
3. **Affiliate links**: Verify quarterly — CJ/Awin links can expire or change; dead links kill trust and conversions
4. **New guides**: Add one per major holiday at least 6 weeks before the date for SEO lead time
5. **CTAs**: Always link to `/demo?ref=guide` (not `/signup`) — lower friction, higher conversion

How to Pick the Right Food Gift

A few rules of thumb that separate a good food gift from a forgettable one:

Match the formality. zChocolat in a mahogany box says "I thought about this." A Ghirardelli Pick & Mix says "I know you like chocolate and I respect your taste." Both are valid — just match the occasion.

Subscriptions beat one-time gifts for close relationships. If this is someone you see regularly, a monthly club means you get to hear about it every time a new box arrives. That is months of goodwill from a single purchase.

When in doubt, chocolate over everything. Cheese has dietary restrictions. Wine has age restrictions. Chocolate is basically universal. Start there if you are unsure.
